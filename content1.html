Origin and History

The Bumbaran, also known as Lake Lanao


Is what the Maranao people inhabited in Mindanao, it is one of the ancient lakes in the world and the deepest and Second largest in the Philippines. The lake is the home range of the Maranao People which is located in the North Central of Mindanao. What is the Origin of the term Maranao? The term Maranao translates to mean "People of the Lake", after their traditional territory in the area sorrounding Lake Lanao in the Bukidnon-Lanao Plateau. This term is generally referred to the native people living around the lake Lanao. The Maranao is, in fact, among the biggest Islamic communities in the Philippines alongside other tribes of the Bangsamoro such as the Maguindanaon and tausug. The Original endonym of the ancestral Maranao is believed to be "Iranaon" that soon became Maguindanao and the Iranun people.
